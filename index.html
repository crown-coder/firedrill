ensuring the safety of our communities.

How It Works

Report a Fire

If you spot a fire, simply click on the "Report Fire Incident" button and fill out the necessary details. Your report will be instantly relayed to the nearest fire department.

Track Your Report

Once you've submitted a report, you can track its status in the "My Reports" section. Stay updated on the actions being taken to address the incident.

Real-Time Response

Our system alerts fire authorities in real-time, enabling a swift response to minimize damage and protect lives.

Enjoy Amazing Features

User-Friendly Reporting

Easily report fires with detailed forms that capture crucial information such as location, severity, and type of fire.

Data Analysis

Access historical data and generate insightful reports to improve fire response strategies and resource allocation.

Incident Dashboard

Authorities can monitor reports with an intuitive dashboard that provides real-time updates and visual data
